**Effective date:** March 26, 2026

## How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your KeepValid account directly from the app:

1. Open KeepValid on your device.
2. Tap the **Account** icon.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

- **Account information** — your email address and authentication credentials
- **All documents** — document names, types, expiration dates, and notes
- **Notification settings** — your reminder preferences and schedules
- **Notification history** — all past notification records
- **Device tokens** — Firebase Cloud Messaging identifiers used for push notifications

## What We Cannot Delete

- **Crash and error reports** already sent to Sentry — these contain only technical diagnostics (device type, OS version, stack traces) and no personal or document data. Sentry retains this data per their [retention policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/).
- **Emails already delivered** to your inbox — we stop sending new notifications immediately, but previously sent emails remain in your email account.
